If you are a small business owner, creator, or front-end designer who occasionally has to handle marketing, you know the struggle: keeping a consistent brand across all your social media posts is incredibly time-consuming.
Enter **Pomelli**, an exciting new experimental tool developed by Google Labs in collaboration with DeepMind. It’s designed specifically to act as an AI marketing co-pilot that _understands_ your brand's unique style.

<ContentBlock>
#### What makes Pomelli special?

Unlike generic text-to-image generators, Pomelli focuses on what it calls your **"Business DNA."**

</ContentBlock>

<ContentBlock>
	#### Here is how it simplifies the workflow:

    <FlowSteps>
    	<FlowStep icon="🧬" title="Analyzes Your Business DNA">
    		It scans your existing website or assets to understand your specific color palettes, typography,
    		image style, and brand tone.
    	</FlowStep>
    	<FlowStep icon="🎨" title="Generates Cohesive Assets">
    		Instead of random, disconnected images, Pomelli generates marketing assets that actually look
    		like they belong to your brand, ready for social media.
    	</FlowStep>
    	<FlowStep icon="✍️" title="Editable Outputs">
    		The generated content isn't a locked image. You can tweak and edit the results to make sure
    		every post aligns perfectly with your marketing goals.
    	</FlowStep>
    </FlowSteps>

</ContentBlock>

Currently available as a **free public beta** through Google Labs, Pomelli aims to level the playing field. It gives small teams and solo creators access to the kind of brand-consistent asset generation that usually requires a full design team.

If you are looking for ways to speed up your content creation without sacrificing your brand's visual identity, Pomelli is definitely worth a look.
