Welcome! If you've arrived here, you're probably curious about the world of Artificial Intelligence (AI). In this guide, we'll explore what these "prompts" everyone is talking about are and how you can use them starting today.

## What exactly is a "Prompt"?

Think of a **Prompt** as a set of instructions or a "recipe" that you give to an AI (like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E) so it knows exactly what you want it to create.

The more detail you give it, the better the result will be. That's why the prompts I share might seem long or complicated—they are designed to get professional and specific results!
