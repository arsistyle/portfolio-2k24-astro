If you've noticed the **Nano Banana** category on the blog, you might be wondering: "What's special about these and how do I use them?"

## Nano Banana: Specialized Creativity

These prompts are specifically optimized to work across a variety of AI tools, but they shine especially well in platforms that support advanced text-to-image and creative generation.

You can use these prompts in:

- **[Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/)**: Google's powerful AI.
- **[Flow](https://labs.google/image-flow/)**: For streamlined creative workflows.
- Any other app that supports standard AI prompting!

### How to get started

	<FlowStep icon="🍌" title="Find a Nano Banana Prompt">
